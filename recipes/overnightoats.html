Reese's Cup Overnight Oats

Photo: Reese's Cup Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup quick oats
  • 1/2 cup chocolate protein shake
  • 1/4 cup high-protein plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar-free peanut butter syrup
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate chips
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter

Steps

  1. Combine oats, protein shake, Greek yogurt and peanut butter syrup in a mason jar with a tight-fitting lid and stir well. Close the lid and refrigerate overnight.
  2. Add chocolate chips to a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on high in 30 second intervals, stirring until smooth. Pour melted chocolate on top of oats. Add peanut butter in small dollops, and swirl into melted chocolate.
